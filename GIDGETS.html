The Gidgets


The Gidgets is a female Vocal Surf Band from a city called Nijmegen in The Netherlands. The group consists of Latifa Ourahou (November 14, 1994), Simone van Elk (September 2, 1995) and Meghan Detrick (January 19, 1995).

They met each other at High School but started singing after they became really good friends. The first beats they made weren't surf at all. They liked hard rock and metal but after they heard the Beach Boys & The Honeys, they were sold. That's when they started singing surf with its scat-singing and tight harmonies on a song's chorus.

The name: The Gidgets comes from the combination of the words "girl" and "midget", and so it means feminine and small. In the surfsport they also call small female surfers Gidgets.
