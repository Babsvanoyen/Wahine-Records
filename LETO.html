Leto

Julie Janssens (Berchem, 17 March 1995), aka Leto is a Belgian artist who travels the world with her instrumental beats. Leto got her artist name from The Goddess of Darkness. She reflects the beauty of the night. Leto is a girl who likes to laugh and who is always in for an adventure. 5 years ago she went on a journey to discover other cultures. During her tour of Australia she came in contact with the surf sound which the young Australians cherished at that time. She immediately lost her heart to the enthusiastic beat of this genre. Her love and passion for this genre were reinforced by the many possibilities this genre had. Leto started experimenting with the surf sound and soon discovered that making instrumental surf beats fitted her like a glove.
